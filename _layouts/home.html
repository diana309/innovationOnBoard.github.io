About Innovation OnBoard Platform

Begin your journey in venture design; Learn, Innovate, Compete

Established in 2017, Innovation Onboard (IOB) is a Canada-wide student-led initiative that fosters entrepreneurship and interdisciplinary collaboration among science, engineering, and business students. This 6-month program includes 10 workshops, lectures, an innovation fair, and a pitch competition with cash prizes.
